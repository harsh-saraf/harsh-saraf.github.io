About

Product and Pitch

Hi, I'm Harsh. I combine an engineering foundation from IIT Kharagpur, strategy training at BCG, and product experience across Abbott, Wordsworth AI, and Intuitive. My work spans digital health, medical devices, and consumer software.

I like solving hard problems and turning ambiguity into clear product decisions. I’ve worked on both software and hardware — from surgeon-facing tools and patient programs to internal AI workflows and consumer-facing digital products.
